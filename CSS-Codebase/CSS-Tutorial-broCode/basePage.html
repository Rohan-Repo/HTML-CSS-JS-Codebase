CSS Tutorial

HTML(Structure) is used to define the structure & content of a webpage Eg. Skeleton or Frame of our House

CSS(Style) is used to give styles or presentation to that structure Eg. Color, Decoration or Lighting in our House

JS(Functionality) is used to give functionality to that Structure Eg. Gives functionality like Plumbing, Electricity, Heating
